Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our online examination system. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us.

Information Collection:

Registration: To use our system, you may be required to provide your email address for authentication and account creation purposes. We do not collect any personally identifiable information without your consent. Password Security: We use secure methods to hash and store your passwords using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms, such as SHA256. This ensures that your passwords remain secure and protected.

Use of Information:

Personal Information: We only use your email address for authentication, account management, and communication purposes related to the online examination system. We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent. Test Results: The system generates result reports based on your test performance, which are only accessible to you and authorized administrators. We do not share your results with any third parties unless required by law.

Data Security:

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. This includes encryption, secure transmission protocols, and restricted access controls. We regularly update our systems and employ best practices to ensure the security and integrity of your data. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links:

Our online examination system does not include any third-party links or integrations. We strive to provide a secure and self-contained environment for your assessments, ensuring that your data remains within our system and under our control.

User Rights:

You have the right to access, modify, and delete your personal information stored in our system. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the provided contact information below.

Please review this privacy policy periodically, as we may update it to reflect any changes in our practices or legal requirements. By using our online examination system, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
